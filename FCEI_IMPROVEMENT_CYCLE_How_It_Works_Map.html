Improvement Cycle hover banners

Hover (or keyboard-focus) any item above or the cards below. A temporary banner shows its mapped Improvement Cycle stage. The banner text is painted to a canvas with an FCEI watermark — try to select it, right-click it, or drag it: you can't, and it isn't in the page's text/DOM.

To wire it on fcei.eu (no rebuild): paste the <style> and <script> blocks into the site, then map existing items either by adding data-fcei-cycle="01" … "05" (or see-it … refine-it) to the elements, or by listing CSS selectors in the CYCLE_SELECTORS map at the top of the script. New items rendered later are picked up automatically.

FCEI Finnish Teacher Training Development Framework

How the Improvement Cycle works on the platform

The cycle guides every learner, teacher, trainer and school team through one clear improvement journey: identify the real issue, apply one manageable Finnish-inspired practice, collect evidence, reflect on impact and refine the next step before scaling.

01

See it

Use evidence to locate the real classroom, workplace or institutional problem without blame.

02

Apply it

Try one practical routine, tool or strategy in one real learning setting first.

03

Evidence it

Upload visible artefacts that prove the change happened and show learner response.

04

Reflect it

Review what improved, what stayed the same and what the evidence suggests next.

05

Refine it

Improve the routine and scale only when evidence supports wider use.

What the participant experiences

  • They begin with a diagnostic task, observation or gap-analysis prompt.
  • They choose one manageable classroom, TVET, leadership or wellbeing action.
  • They upload proof such as learner work, checklists, photos, rubrics or reflections.
  • They complete a guided impact review using evidence, not opinion only.
  • They revise the practice and decide whether to repeat, adapt or scale it.
