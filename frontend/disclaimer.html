1. Research-Inspired, Not Official Finnish Government Certification

The Finnish Paradigm™ provides professional development courses, toolkits, and resources that are inspired by and informed by research into Finnish education principles. Our content draws on peer-reviewed research, published educational frameworks, and evidence-based pedagogical practices that have been associated with Finnish educational success.

The Finnish Paradigm™ is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or certified by the Finnish government, the Finnish National Agency for Education (EDUFI), or any official Finnish educational authority, unless a specific course or programme explicitly states otherwise with reference to a formal accreditation or partnership agreement.

Our use of "Finnish" in our name and materials refers to the research tradition and educational philosophy that inspires our approach. It does not imply official Finnish governmental approval, certification, or accreditation.

2. Certificates and Qualifications

Certificates issued by The Finnish Paradigm™ are CPD (Continuing Professional Development) certificates that recognise the completion of our professional development programmes. They are not:

  • Academic qualifications (degrees, diplomas, or formally assessed academic credits).
  • Teaching licences or qualified teacher status (QTS) certifications.
  • Government-accredited professional certifications.
  • Formal Finnish education qualifications.

Where a specific course has been formally accredited by an external body, this will be clearly stated on the course listing page, including the name of the accrediting body and the scope of the accreditation.

The recognition and value of our CPD certificates may vary by employer, jurisdiction, and professional body. Users should verify with their employer or relevant professional body whether our CPD certificates meet their specific requirements.

3. No Guaranteed Outcomes

The Finnish Paradigm™ provides evidence-informed professional development designed to support teachers and school leaders in improving their practice. However:

  • We do not guarantee specific outcomes for students, classrooms, or schools as a result of completing our courses or implementing our strategies.
  • Educational outcomes depend on a wide range of factors beyond the scope of any single professional development programme, including school context, student demographics, resources, leadership, and individual implementation.
  • Research findings referenced in our courses represent general trends and evidence from specific studies, which may not be directly replicable in all educational contexts.
  • Assessment scores and progress data generated through the platform reflect performance on our specific instruments and should be interpreted within that context.

Users are responsible for exercising their own professional judgement when applying strategies and approaches learned through our courses to their specific educational context.

  • Video presentations featuring AI-generated presenters or narration.

5. Professional Development Context

All content provided through The Finnish Paradigm™ is intended solely for professional development purposes within an educational context. Our content does not constitute:

  • Legal advice: Information about education law, safeguarding regulations, or data protection is provided for general awareness only. Schools should consult qualified legal advisors for specific legal questions.
  • Medical or clinical advice: Content relating to Special Educational Needs (SEN), learning difficulties, or student wellbeing is intended for general professional awareness and does not replace clinical assessment, diagnosis, or treatment by qualified medical professionals.
  • Psychological assessment or diagnosis: Our assessment instruments are designed for educational screening and progress monitoring, not for clinical diagnosis.
  • Mandatory regulatory compliance guidance: While our courses may reference regulatory frameworks (e.g., Ofsted, GDPR, safeguarding legislation), this is for educational context only and does not constitute compliance advice.
