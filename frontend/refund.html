Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

The Finnish Paradigm™ offers digital products and services, including online CPD courses, assessment instruments, toolkit resources, and subscription plans. Because our products are delivered digitally and access is granted immediately upon purchase, our refund policy reflects the nature of digital goods delivery.

We aim to be fair and transparent in all refund decisions. This policy sets out the circumstances in which refunds are available and how to request one.

2. Individual Course Purchases

2.1 Cooling-Off Period (14 Days)

If you have purchased a course but have not yet started it (i.e., you have not accessed any course modules, watched any video content, or attempted any assessments), you may request a full refund within 14 days of purchase.

This cooling-off period is in line with consumer distance-selling regulations and applies regardless of the reason for your request.

2.2 Partial Completion

If you have started a course but completed less than 50% of the total course content (measured by modules accessed and assessments attempted), you may request a refund within 14 days of purchase. In this case, we will assess the request on a case-by-case basis and may offer:

  • A full refund, or
  • A partial refund proportional to the content not yet accessed, or
  • Credit towards another course of equal or lesser value.

2.3 No Refund After Substantial Completion

Once you have completed more than 50% of a course's content, or once a CPD certificate has been issued for the course, no refund will be provided. At this point, the substantial benefit of the digital product has been delivered.

2.4 Downloadable Resources and Toolkits

Downloadable digital resources (toolkit PDFs, intervention templates, assessment instruments) are non-refundable once downloaded, as the full product has been delivered at that point.

3. Subscription Plans

If you are on a subscription plan (monthly or annual):

  • Cancellation: You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard or by contacting us. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, and you will retain access until that date.
  • Pro-rata refunds: If you cancel an annual subscription within the first 30 days and have accessed fewer than 3 courses, you may be eligible for a pro-rata refund for the unused portion of your subscription period.
  • Monthly subscriptions: Monthly plans are not eligible for partial-month refunds. You may cancel to prevent future charges, but the current month's fee is non-refundable.
  • Free trial periods: If a free trial is offered, you will not be charged if you cancel before the trial period ends. Once the trial converts to a paid subscription, standard subscription refund terms apply.

4. School and Institutional Licences

Refund terms for school and institutional licences are negotiated separately as part of the licence agreement. Standard terms typically include:

  • A 30-day satisfaction review period from the date of licence activation.
  • Pro-rata refunds for unused licence seats if the institution reduces its requirements within the first quarter.
  • No refund for fully utilised licence seats where teachers have completed courses or accessed resources.

If you are a school or institutional licence holder and wish to discuss refund terms, please contact us directly at the details provided in Section 8.

5. Exceptional Circumstances

We may consider refund requests outside the standard policy in exceptional circumstances, including:

  • Significant technical issues that prevent access to purchased content and cannot be resolved by our support team within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Duplicate or accidental purchases (please contact us within 48 hours).
  • Course content that is materially different from what was described on the listing page at the time of purchase.

Each exceptional case will be reviewed individually, and our decision will be final.

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please:

  1. Email us at [refunds@thefinnishparadigm.com - to be confirmed] with the subject line "Refund Request".
  2. Include your account email address, the course or product name, date of purchase, and reason for the refund request.
  3. Alternatively, use our contact form and select "Refund Request" as the enquiry type.

We aim to acknowledge refund requests within 2 business days and provide a decision within 5 business days.

7. Payment Processing and Timelines

All payments and refunds are processed through Stripe, our third-party payment processor.

  • Once a refund is approved, it will be initiated through Stripe within 3 business days.
  • Refunds are returned to the original payment method used at purchase.
  • Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.
  • We do not process refunds by alternative payment methods (e.g., bank transfer, cheque, or cryptocurrency).

If you have not received your refund within 15 business days of approval, please contact your bank or card issuer first, then contact us if the issue remains unresolved.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Refund Policy or wish to request a refund, please contact us:

  • Email: [refunds@thefinnishparadigm.com - to be confirmed]
  • Contact form: Contact page

This policy should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before commercial launch.
