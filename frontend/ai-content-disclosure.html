1. Purpose

FCEI may use AI-assisted tools and AI-avatar technology to support professional development delivery. This page explains how AI and synthetic media may appear on the platform.

2. AI tools FCEI may use

FCEI may use AI or automation for:

  • AI-avatar video presentation;
  • synthetic voice or voice enhancement;
  • captions and transcript support;
  • course script drafting and editing;
  • slide or visual production support;
  • translation support;
  • summarisation;
  • learning activity generation;
  • customer-support assistance;
  • analytics and course improvement.

3. AI-avatar video disclosure

Some training videos may include AI-generated avatars, synthetic voices, automated captions, edited media or AI-assisted instructional presentation.

Required disclosure under each relevant video:


This training video may include AI-generated avatar presentation, synthetic voice, captions or edited instructional media. The educational content is produced and reviewed by FCEI for professional development purposes.

4. Human review

AI-assisted materials should be reviewed before publication for:

  • educational accuracy;
  • safeguarding risk;
  • copyright issues;
  • misleading accreditation claims;
  • bias, stereotypes or discriminatory language;
  • privacy and personal-data risk;
  • alignment with FCEI curriculum.

5. No impersonation or deception

FCEI does not permit AI tools to be used for:

  • impersonating real people without permission;
  • creating deceptive deepfakes;
  • fake endorsements;
  • misleading accreditation claims;
  • harassment;
  • fraud;
  • unlawful surveillance;
  • exploiting minors;
  • creating illegal or harmful content.

6. User uploads to AI systems

Users must not upload another person’s face, voice, image, child data, learner record, confidential school information, copyrighted material or personal data into an AI tool unless they have permission and a lawful basis.

7. Third-party AI providers

FCEI may use approved AI providers for video, media or platform support. These providers should be listed in the subprocessor register where they process personal data for FCEI.

8. AI limitations

AI-assisted content may contain mistakes. FCEI training content is for professional development and must be adapted to local law, school policy, safeguarding requirements and curriculum standards.
