1. Purpose

This Refund and Cancellation Policy explains when users may cancel purchases, request refunds or stop subscriptions for FCEI digital courses, downloadable resources, live workshops, consultancy services, institutional packages and subscriptions.

2. Product categories

FCEI sells or may sell:

  • online self-paced courses;
  • downloadable resources and templates;
  • live online workshops;
  • in-person workshops;
  • institutional training packages;
  • school consultancy services;
  • subscriptions or membership access;
  • certificate or assessment services where applicable.

4. Immediate digital access consent

At checkout, users must actively confirm if they want immediate access to digital content. The checkout should state that immediate access may affect cancellation rights after streaming, downloading or starting course use.

5. Downloadable resources

Downloadable PDFs, templates, scripts, toolkits and documents are usually non-refundable once downloaded, unless the file is defective, incorrectly supplied or local law requires otherwise.

7. Consultancy services

Consultancy fees may include preparation, diagnosis, planning, meetings, document review, training delivery, travel, expert time and implementation support.

Where work has already started, FCEI may deduct reasonable fees for work completed, preparation time, committed third-party costs and non-refundable expenses. Institutional consultancy should be governed by a signed proposal or contract.

8. Subscriptions

Users may cancel future renewals through the account dashboard or by contacting support. Cancelling a subscription stops future billing but does not automatically refund past periods unless required by law or stated in the plan.

9. Duplicate payments and billing errors

Duplicate payments, confirmed billing errors or incorrect charges should be refunded once verified.
