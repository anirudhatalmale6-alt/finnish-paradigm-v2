1. Purpose

This Policy protects FCEI users, staff, institutions, learners, intellectual property, platform security and legal compliance.

2. Permitted use

Users may access FCEI content for personal professional development, institutional learning, school improvement, training participation or authorised consultancy use according to their purchased plan or institutional licence.

3. Prohibited use

Users must not:

  • share login credentials;
  • copy, resell or redistribute FCEI content;
  • upload FCEI documents to public file-sharing sites;
  • remove watermarks or copyright notices;
  • scrape, crawl or mass-download content;
  • bypass payment or access controls;
  • upload malware or harmful code;
  • harass, abuse, threaten or discriminate;
  • impersonate another person or institution;
  • make false accreditation claims;
  • upload unlawful content;
  • upload child images or learner data without permission;
  • upload copyrighted material without rights;
  • use AI tools for deception, impersonation or harmful synthetic media;
  • use the platform for spam, phishing, fraud or unlawful marketing.

4. Copyright ownership

FCEI owns or licenses its course materials, videos, scripts, slides, downloads, PDFs, frameworks, templates, assessments, curriculum maps, branding and platform layout.

Users receive only the licence expressly granted with their course, product or institutional package.

5. User-generated content

You retain ownership of content you upload, but you grant FCEI a limited licence to store, process, review and use it for course delivery, assessment, feedback, evidence verification, institutional reporting and compliance.

You are responsible for ensuring uploaded content is lawful, accurate, non-confidential where appropriate, and authorised for upload.
