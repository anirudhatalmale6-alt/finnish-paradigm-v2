3. Personal data we collect

We may collect:

Account data

Name, email address, password hash, country, organisation, job title, profile information, account status and login method.

Learning data

Course enrolments, progress, quiz scores, completion status, assignments, reflections, uploaded evidence, feedback, certificates and learning records.

Payment and billing data

Billing name, billing address, payment status, transaction ID, invoice records, refund status, coupon use, tax/VAT information and payment-provider identifiers. FCEI does not intentionally store full card numbers or card security codes.

Communication data

Support requests, emails, contact forms, workshop enquiries, consultation requests, complaints and survey responses.

Technical and security data

IP address, device type, browser, operating system, login logs, session logs, cookie choices, audit logs, error logs and security events.

Marketing data

Marketing opt-in status, email preferences, campaign source, unsubscribe records and engagement with FCEI communications.

AI/video interaction data

Video module access, AI-avatar content viewed, captions/transcripts accessed, learning interactions and analytics connected to professional development use.

Institutional data

Organisation name, administrator details, learner lists, enrolments, cohort progress, reporting settings, contracts and Data Processing Addendum records.

4. How we collect data

We collect data when you:

  • visit the website;
  • create an account;
  • buy a course or subscription;
  • join an institutional cohort;
  • complete quizzes, assignments or evidence tasks;
  • upload files;
  • request support;
  • accept policies;
  • manage cookie settings;
  • subscribe or unsubscribe from emails;
  • attend live or recorded training;
  • use FCEI AI-supported or video features.

6. Special category data and children’s data

FCEI does not require users to upload health data, biometric data, sensitive safeguarding records, political opinions, religious information, trade-union data or other special category data. Users must not upload such data unless they have a lawful basis and explicit permission.

FCEI is primarily designed for adult professional learners. Where children or learners under 18 may access the platform, the Safeguarding and Children’s Data Policy applies.

7. Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies according to the Cookie Policy. Non-essential analytics, marketing and third-party media cookies should not be activated until valid consent is given where required.

8. Sharing personal data

We may share limited data with:

  • hosting and database providers;
  • payment processors;
  • video hosting providers;
  • AI-avatar and media-production providers where relevant;
  • email and communication providers;
  • analytics providers where consent applies;
  • institutional administrators where you are enrolled through an institution;
  • trainers, assessors or support staff involved in course delivery;
  • regulators, courts, tax authorities or law enforcement where legally required.

9. Subprocessors

A public subprocessor register should be maintained at `/subprocessors`. The register should state the provider, purpose, data processed, country, privacy link and transfer mechanism.

10. International transfers

Where personal data is transferred internationally, FCEI will use appropriate safeguards where required, such as adequacy decisions, contractual safeguards, Data Processing Agreements or equivalent mechanisms.

13. Marketing communications

We only send marketing emails where we have consent or another lawful basis. Every marketing email must include an unsubscribe option. Transactional emails such as receipts, password resets, course-access messages and policy notices may still be sent where necessary.

14. Security

FCEI uses reasonable technical and organisational measures including HTTPS, access controls, password hashing, admin MFA, audit logs, secure file storage and restricted role permissions. No online platform can guarantee absolute security.
