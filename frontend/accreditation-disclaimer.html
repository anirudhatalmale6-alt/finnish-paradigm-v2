1. FCEI status

Finland Creative Education Institute / FCEI provides Finnish-inspired professional development, school improvement, curriculum, TVET, leadership, consultancy and training services.

2. No automatic official accreditation

Unless expressly stated in writing, FCEI courses and certificates are not:

  • Finnish government qualifications;
  • Finnish National Agency for Education certificates;
  • FINEEC accreditations;
  • university degrees;
  • teaching licences;
  • statutory professional licences;
  • immigration qualifications;
  • employment guarantees;
  • school inspection approvals.

3. Use of Finnish-inspired language

FCEI may refer to Finnish-inspired education, Finnish pedagogy, Finnish education values, international education research, equity, trust, wellbeing, learner agency, formative assessment, early support, mixed-ability teaching, TVET and school improvement.

These references are educational and comparative. They do not create official Finnish government endorsement.

4. Certificates

FCEI certificates may confirm:

  • participation;
  • completion;
  • learning hours;
  • assessment completion;
  • evidence upload;
  • consultancy participation;
  • workshop attendance.

A certificate confirms only the information printed on the certificate and visible on the certificate verification page.

5. Certificate wording required

Every certificate must include:


This certificate confirms completion of the named FCEI professional development course. It is not a government-issued qualification, teaching licence, university degree, or official Finnish state accreditation unless expressly stated in a separate formal accreditation agreement.

6. Institutional claims

Schools, training centres, partners and learners must not market FCEI certificates as official Finnish state qualifications unless FCEI provides written permission and verified accreditation documents.
