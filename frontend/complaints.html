1. Purpose

This Policy explains how users, learners, institutions, purchasers, parents, trainers and partners can raise complaints about FCEI services.

2. What can be complained about

You may complain about:

  • course access;
  • payment or refund handling;
  • certificate errors;
  • content accuracy;
  • accessibility barriers;
  • privacy handling;
  • trainer conduct;
  • consultancy delivery;
  • institutional reporting;
  • technical problems;
  • safeguarding concerns;
  • copyright concerns;
  • misleading information.
